Determining distribution of lists

Following the guide lines developed in Pieper et al.Â (2022), participants are assessed with respect to reaction times and answers towards control items, which may lead to an exclusion of participants. In EXP 2, this led to an uneven distribution of the lists. This does not affect the modelling of the data.

Random slope model for full data set

               data = data, family = "binomial")
